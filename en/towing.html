Our Equipment

Our tow truck is equipped with a drop-deck flatbed platform 6.0 m × 2.55 m, a hydraulic winch for pulling vehicles that cannot move on their own, and a crane with a lifting capacity of 5,400 kg and a reach of 10.4 m. This combination allows us to handle virtually any recovery scenario — from a compact car with a dead battery to a bus stuck in a ditch.

What We Tow

  • Passenger cars and SUVs
  • Vans and minibuses
  • Buses and coaches
  • Trucks and heavy vehicles
  • Agricultural machinery (tractors, trailers)

Where We Tow To

We can tow your vehicle to any destination — a local repair shop in Dobrich or Varna, a specific address, or for long-distance repatriation across Bulgaria and to other European countries. If you are a tourist whose rental car broke down, we can arrange transport to the nearest authorised service centre. Call us and describe your situation — we will find the best solution.
