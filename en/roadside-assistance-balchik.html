Roadside Assistance in Balchik

Balchik's old town is built on steep terraced streets above the sea — narrow, winding roads that can catch out unfamiliar drivers. The main coastal Road 9 connects Balchik southward to Varna and northward to Kavarna. Balchik marina and the Botanical Garden attract large numbers of tourists in summer, including many from Romania who drive in via the E87. Overheating on steep town roads, punctures on the rocky coastal path, and parking incidents near the marina are common call-out reasons.

Our Services in Balchik

We cover Balchik town centre, the marina, the Botanical Garden area, and surrounding villages: Obrochishte, Trigortsi and Hrabrovo. On steep road sections, our crane (5,400 kg) is essential for safely loading vehicles that cannot self-load onto the flatbed. We tow to repair shops in Balchik, Dobrich (40 km) or Varna (40 km). Response time: approximately 30 minutes.
