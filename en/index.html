Who We Are

SISI Towing (Пътна помощ СИСИ ЕООД) is a professional roadside assistance company based in Dobrich, Bulgaria. We operate 24 hours a day, 7 days a week — including nights, weekends and public holidays. Whether you are a tourist visiting Albena or Kranevo, a business driver on the E87 coastal road, or a truck operator crossing the Romanian border at Silistra, we are here to help.

Our Services

  • Vehicle towing — flatbed truck 6.0 m × 2.55 m, for cars, vans, minibuses and buses
  • Crane services — up to 5,400 kg / 10.4 m reach, for heavy vehicles and difficult recoveries
  • Jump start — dead battery on the spot
  • Tire change — roadside tire swap for all vehicle types
  • Locksmith — locked keys inside the vehicle
  • Long-distance towing — anywhere in Bulgaria and across Europe

Coverage Area

We cover the entire Northeast Bulgaria region, including:

  • Dobrich — our base, 30 min response
  • Varna — Black Sea city, port, airport
  • Albena — resort complex on E87
  • Kranevo — family resort on E87
  • Balchik — coastal town with marina
  • Kavarna — northernmost Black Sea town, near Romania border
